Our Brand


The Akwaaba Care Services Logo is a composition of the African Adinkra Symbol and a leaf (which represents life, care or treatment). The Adinkra symbol is known as Akwaaba in the Akan dilect which simply means ‘Welcome” and is an important expression in Ghanaian custom because it symbolizes acceptance, friendliness and cooperation. These reflect the manner in which our care services are delivered to our clients and in effect bringing life and refreshment just as the leaf signifies. From a varied perspective, the logo may somewhat appear as a cross-sectional view of an apple. A fruit that promises numerous health benefits just as our services do.

Akwaaba Care Aims and Objectives

01.

To ensure that the care service is delivered flexibly, attentively, and in a non-discriminatory fashion while respecting each service user’s right to independence, privacy, dignity, fulfillment, and the right to make informed choices and take risks.

02.

To respect the diversity of people who use our service and staff. We honor the diversity of our users and staff, creating an inclusive environment where every unique perspective is valued. Our commitment to diversity is at the heart of our community. Together, we celebrate differences and foster unity.

03.

To ensure that agreed care contracts deliver the care service. Our priority is to translate agreed care contracts into impeccable care services, meeting our clients' expectations consistently. We are unwavering in our commitment to deliver on our promises

04.

To manage and implement a formal programme of staff planning, selection, recruitment, training, and personal development to enable the people who use our services to benefit from services delivered by well-trained, skilled, and experienced staff.

05.

To manage the care service efficiently and effectively to make the best use of resources and to maximise value for money for the service user.
